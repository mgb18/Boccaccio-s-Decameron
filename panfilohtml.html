Panfilo

Panfilo is in charge of Day Ten, where he dictates the last theme, which are stories that end happily. He narrates the First Story in Day One, the Seventh Story in Day Two, the Fourth Story in Day Three, the Sixth Story in Day Four, the First Story in Day Five, the Sixth Story in Day Six, the Nineth Story in Day Seven, the Second Story in Day Eight, and the Sixth Story in Day Nine.
