Panfilo

Description

Panfilo is a lover of everything, but he also shows the need to look deep into someone's actions by telling stories with characters that hide their true intentions. Panfilo is the voice of Boccaccio and so Panfilo's suggestion to judge characters fully to find a true meaning mirrors Boccaccio's suggestion to readers: it is necessary to look into the stories for advice. Panfilo is in charge of Day Ten, where he dictates the last theme, which are stories that end happily.
