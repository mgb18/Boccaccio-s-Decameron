Panfilo

Based on our charts, we could say a number of things. One, that Boccaccio tends to portray women in a somewhat more positive light then men, by making them less violent, more clever, and honest. However, the depiction of women as being more passive and naive tends to play into the stereotypical view of women. More can be said after futhre examination, just some preliminary theories.
