Lauretta

Lauretta dictates the theme of Day Eight, which is of men and women playing tricks on each other. She narrates the Eighth Story in Day One, the Fourth Story in Day Two, the Eighth Story in Day Three, the Third Story in Day Four, the Seventh Story in Day Five, the Third Story in Day Six, the Fourth Story in Day Seven, the Nineth Story in Day Eight, the Eighth Story in Day Nine, and the Fourth Story in Day Ten.
