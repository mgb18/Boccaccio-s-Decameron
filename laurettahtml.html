Lauretta

Description

Lauretta is the storyteller who reminds the reader of the harsh sexist realities of the 14th century; she believes that women must obey men at all costs. Her stories counter those featuring assertive women. Lauretta dictates the theme of Day Eight, which is of men and women playing tricks on each other.
