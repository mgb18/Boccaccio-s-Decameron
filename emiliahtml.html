Emilia

Emilia is in charge of Day Nine, but she perscribes no single theme for the day. She narrates the Sixth Story in Day One, the Sixth Story in Day Two, the Seventh Story in Day Three, the Seventh Story in Day Four, the Second Story in Day Five, the Eighth Story in Day Six, the First Story in Day Seven, the Fourth Story in Day Eight, the Ninth Story in Day Nine, and the Fifth Story in Day Ten.
