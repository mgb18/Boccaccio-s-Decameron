Fiammetta

Fiammetta dictates the theme of Day Five, which consists of lovers that go through difficult situations that ultimately end happily. She narrates the Fifth Story in Day One, the Fifth Story in Day Two, the Sixth Story in Day Three, the First Story in Day Four, the Nineth Story in Day Five, the Sixth Story in Day Six, the Fifth Story in Day Seven, the Eighth Story in Day Eight, the Nineth Story in Day Nine, and the Sixth Story in Day Ten.
