Research Question

This is a project that is based on the notion that we can use Digital Humanities to help us determine whether or not The Decameron is a feminist work. Although it's difficult to quantify exactly what makes a work "feminist" we believe that by marking up the text in a way that will help us quantify the behavior of the characters. Specifically, we plan on contrasting the behavior of the men against the behavior of the women. We say "behavior" because we are marking up both actions and speech. Through marking up the types of things the men and women of The Decameron do or say, we'll be able to better evaluate whether or not we can truly say that this is a "feminist" text.
